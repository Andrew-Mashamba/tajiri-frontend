Now: What Should TAJIRI Do?

We can combine the best of all three. Which hybrid approach appeals to you?

A

Adaptive Zones (Recommended)

Instagram range (4:5 to 1.91:1) + TikTok letterbox for extremes + dominant color blur bg.
In-range images: display at natural ratio with BoxFit.cover (Instagram-style).
Too-tall (< 4:5): show at 4:5 container but with blurred+scaled background behind the full image (TikTok-inspired, no content loss).
Too-wide (> 1.91:1): show at 1.91:1 with dominant-color bands or blurred bg (no hard crop).
Result: No content ever lost. Feed stays rhythmic. Best of all three.

B

Strict Instagram Clamp

Pure Instagram: clamp to 4:5 - 1.91:1, crop everything else.
Simple. Consistent feed rhythm. But tall screenshots and panoramas lose content permanently in display.
Current implementation. Works but users lose parts of their photos.

C

Content-Aware Hybrid

Detect content type and pick strategy per-image.
Photos/selfies: Instagram clamp + cover crop (faces survive center-crop).
Screenshots/text images: TikTok-style fit (never crop text).
Videos: always natural ratio clamped to 4:5 - 16:9.
Requires backend content classification (face detection, text detection).
Smartest result but needs ML classification from backend.
