Key Design Differences

1

Chapter bars, not "Step X of Y"

4 thick bars at top showing chapters. Current chapter label below. No numbers = no counting.

2

Questions, not form labels

"Ulisoma sekondari wapi?" instead of "Secondary School". Feels like a conversation.

3

Tap chips for years, not dropdowns

Scrollable year chips are faster than opening a dropdown and scrolling. One tap vs three.

4

Contextual skip text, not generic "Ruka"

"Sijasoma sekondari" (I didn't go to secondary) instead of "Skip". Feels respectful, not dismissive.
