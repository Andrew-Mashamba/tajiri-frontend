Section 4: Creator Incentive & Payment System

Hybrid model: Fund Pool (base) + Direct Monetization (tips/subs) + Ad Revenue Share (future)

Revenue Flows to Creators

Tier 1: Creator Fund Pool (Base Income)

How It Works

TAJIRI allocates a monthly fund (configurable, starts at TSh 10M). Every qualifying creator gets a share proportional to their weighted engagement score relative to all other qualifying creators that month.

Low thresholds by design — TAJIRI is for emerging creators, not just superstars. New creators can start earning within their first month.

Engagement-Weighted Formula (Already Built)

Uses the existing creator_earnings_rates table and PostEarningsController:

base_score = (views × view_rate) + (likes × like_rate) + (shares × share_rate) + (saves × save_rate) + (comments × comment_rate) + (watch_seconds × watch_rate)

Rates are configurable in the backend table. Shares and saves weighted highest — they indicate genuine value.

Community Bonus 1.0x — 1.5x

Reply to 50%+ of comments on a post → 1.5x on that post's earnings. Measured per-post, not globally. Encourages creators to engage with their audience on every post, not just popular ones.

Virality Bonus 1.0x — 5.0x

Post triggers a gossip thread → 2x. Thread reaches 50+ posts → 3x. Thread reaches 200+ posts → 5x. This is the jackpot — makes creators think "what will people talk about?" which is exactly what feeds the Flywheel.

= TSh 5,625 for this single post

Tier 2: Direct Monetization (Already Partially Built)

Subscriptions

Already built: subscription tiers, billing, subscriber management. Creator sets price, offers exclusive content to subscribers. Platform takes 15% commission. Payout to creator wallet.

EXISTS — enhance with Flywheel integration
Tips & Gifts

Already built: SendTipScreen, tip models. Viewers send one-time tips to creators. Predefined amounts (TSh 500, 1K, 5K, 10K) + custom. Platform takes 10% commission. Visible on post as "X people tipped this post."

EXISTS — add gossip thread tipping
Livestream Gifts

Already built: StreamSuperChat model, gift system. Real-time gifts during livestream with animated effects. Platform takes 20% commission. Higher commission because livestream infrastructure costs more.

EXISTS — no changes needed
Sponsored Posts (NEW)

Marketplace where businesses pay creators to feature products. Creator sets their rate based on their tier. Post tagged as "Sponsored" with business attribution. Business pays TAJIRI, TAJIRI pays creator (20% commission). Star/Legend tiers only.

NEW — build as part of Flywheel

Tier 3: Ad Revenue Share (Future Phase)

When TAJIRI introduces advertising (in-feed ads, pre-roll on videos), creators get a % of ad revenue generated by views on their content. Split: 55% creator / 45% platform (industry standard). Ads only shown on posts with 1K+ views (protects small creators from low-value ads). Creator can opt out of ads on their content. This layer activates only when the ad system is built — no dependency on launch.

Payout System

Mobile Money First

M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, Halo Pesa. This is Tanzania — mobile money is how people get paid. Minimum payout: TSh 5,000. Processing: within 48 hours. No bank account required.

Payout Schedule

Fund pool: distributed on the 1st of each month. Tips/gifts: available immediately (instant payout). Subscriptions: monthly on renewal date. Sponsored posts: on campaign completion. All visible in the existing Earnings Dashboard.

Transparency Dashboard

Enhance existing Creator Stats Panel and Earnings Dashboard to show: fund pool share calculation (your score / total pool), each multiplier applied with exact values, comparison to previous month, projection for current month based on current trajectory. Creators must trust the system — transparency builds trust.

Creator Journey: From Zero to Legend

Week 1: New Creator

Posts first content. Sees "You earned TSh 12 from 45 views" in Stats Panel. Small but real — proves the system works. Gets "First Post" celebration. Sees path to qualification (100 followers, 4 posts, 1K views).

Month 1: Rising (Qualified)

Hits thresholds, enters fund pool. First real payout: TSh 2,500. Starts posting streak (1.1x after 7 days). Sees which posts earn most — learns what works. Receives first tip from a viewer: TSh 1,000.

Month 3: Star

Creator Score hits 60+. Tier multiplier jumps to 2.0x. First post triggers a gossip thread — 3x virality bonus. Monthly earnings: TSh 45,000+. Opens subscription tier. Gets approached for sponsored post. Weekly performance reports show consistent growth.

Month 6+: Legend

Score 85+. 2.5x tier multiplier. 90-day posting streak (1.5x). Regular gossip thread triggers. Multiple income streams: fund pool (TSh 80K) + subscriptions (TSh 120K) + tips (TSh 30K) + sponsored posts (TSh 200K). This is now a real job. TAJIRI keeps them because no other platform offers this combination.
