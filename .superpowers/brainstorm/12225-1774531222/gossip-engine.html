Section 3: The Gossip-Virality Engine

Algorithm-driven trending + AI-packaged presentation — priority: Global (B) → East African (A) → Hyperlocal (C)

How a Gossip Thread Is Born

1
Velocity Spike Detection

Every 5 minutes, backend calculates engagement velocity for recent posts: (likes + comments×2 + shares×3) / hours_since_posted. Posts exceeding 2x their creator's average velocity are flagged as "heating up."

2
Claude CLI Clusters Related Posts

Claude analyzes flagged posts: same hashtags? Same topic? Same person being discussed? Response posts to each other? Groups them into a gossip_thread. Assigns a seed post (the one that started it) and category tag.

3
Template Title Assignment

Claude picks a title template from a predefined library (bilingual) and fills slots. Example: template "trending_{category}_hot" → "🔥 {category} Imewaka" / "🔥 {category} Is On Fire". Slots: category, city, creator name, count. No freeform text generation.

4
Thread Goes Live

Thread appears in Trending tab, personalized digests, and as inline cards in the main feed. As new related posts appear, Claude adds them to the thread. Thread stays active as long as velocity stays above threshold. Naturally dies when conversation moves on.

The Three Gossip Layers

Priority B → A → C, all running simultaneously

Layer B: Global Trending (Primary)

HIGHEST PRIORITY

Algorithm-driven, platform-wide trending. What the whole TAJIRI community is talking about.

Trending Topics

Hashtag velocity spikes across all users. "#BongoFlava" suddenly gets 500 posts/hour → trending thread created. Shows post count and velocity indicator (🔥 rising, ⚡ exploding).

Viral Chains

When a post gets shared 50+ times in an hour, track the repost chain. Thread shows: original post → top reactions → counter-reactions. "Challenge" posts that spawn response videos auto-thread.

Creator Battles

When two creators post about the same topic with opposing takes, Claude detects the "beef." Creates a split-thread: Side A vs Side B. Users can tap to "take a side" — engagement goldmine.

Milestone Moments

Creator hits 10K followers? First post to break 1M views? Livestream breaks viewer record? Auto-generates a "milestone thread" celebrating the moment. Community rally point.

Layer A: East African Culture (Secondary)

FLAVOR LAYER

Cultural context that makes TAJIRI feel Tanzanian, not generic.

Kumekucha Digest

Morning push notification: "Kumekucha! Hizi ndio habari za leo..." (Dawn has broken! Here's today's news...). Top 3-5 threads personalized to user. Evening version: "Usiku Mwema — here's what you missed." Bilingual based on user's language setting.

Bongo Categories

Thread categories that resonate locally: Bongo Flava (music), Mitumba & Biashara (fashion & business), Michezo (sports), Siasa Light (politics-lite), Burudani (entertainment), Uhusiano (relationships). Category tags drive personalization.

Local Celebrity Tracking

When verified/popular creators are mentioned across multiple posts, Claude detects a "personality trend." Groups all posts about that person. "Everyone is talking about @diamond_platnumz today — 15 posts and counting."

Swahili Proverb of the Day

Daily cultural anchor in the digest. Pre-written library of ~365 Swahili proverbs with English translations. Not AI-generated — curated human content. Creates a daily "tradition" touchpoint. Users share favorites.

Layer C: Hyperlocal (Discovery)

DISCOVERY LAYER

What's happening in your area — powered by the existing "Nearby" feed endpoint.

Neighborhood Threads

Posts geotagged within 5km radius that spike in engagement get threaded. "Kinondoni is buzzing — 8 posts about the new market." Drives foot traffic for marketplace sellers.

Local Business Buzz

When multiple posts mention/tag the same shop or location, create a "Business Spotlight" thread. Bridges social → commerce: "3 people just reviewed Mama Lishe's Kitchen — see what they said." Links to shop page if seller is on TAJIRI.

Community Alerts

High-velocity local posts get "Alert" treatment: traffic, events, weather, marketplace scam warnings. Uses existing engagement velocity detection — anything spiking in a small geographic area gets flagged as potentially important.

Nearby Creator Discovery

Surface creators who are popular in the user's area but the user doesn't follow yet. "5 creators in Mikocheni that 200 of your neighbors follow." Low-pressure follow suggestions tied to geographic community.

How Gossip Appears in the Feed

Not a separate tab — woven into the existing feed experience

Thread Cards in Main Feed

Every 8-12 regular posts, inject a "Gossip Thread Card" showing: thread title, post count, top reaction, velocity indicator. Tap opens the full thread viewer. Card design matches PostCard but with a distinct "thread" visual (stacked cards effect, category color accent).

Trending Tab (Powered by Gossip Engine)

Existing Discover → Trending tab becomes thread-first. Shows top threads ranked by velocity, personalized to user. Individual posts still show, but threads get priority placement. Filter chips: All, Entertainment, Business, Music, Sports, Local.

Thread Viewer Screen

New screen: shows thread title, seed post at top, then related posts in engagement-ranked order. Live counter: "47 new posts since you opened this thread." Pull-to-refresh adds new posts. Swipe left/right between threads. Bottom bar: thread stats (total posts, participants, duration active).

Digest Screen

Opened from morning/evening push notification. Shows 3-5 personalized thread summaries: title, post count, top post preview, "continue reading" CTA. Swahili proverb of the day at top. "Threads you left unfinished" section at bottom. One-tap into any thread.

Safety Rails

Auto-suppress threshold: If a thread gets 10+ reports in 1 hour, auto-hide from trending until manual review.

No hate amplification: Claude CLI checks thread sentiment — threads driven by harassment/hate speech don't get promoted (still visible, just not boosted).

Cooldown: Same topic can't trend more than 3 times in 24 hours (prevents manipulation/spam).

Creator opt-out: Creators can toggle "Don't include my posts in gossip threads" in settings.
