# Messaging & Communication Features

## 1. Core Messaging

### ✉️ Text Messaging
- One-to-one chat
- Group chats
- Message editing after sending
- Unsent message deletion
- Message drafts saved automatically
- Reply, quote, and forward messages

### 🗣️ Voice & Video Messages
- Send voice notes
- Auto-transcription of voice messages to text on device (privacy-preserving)
- In-chat recording with visual indicators
- Video messages (short videos sent like voice notes)
- Missed call message features (send audio/video note if call isn’t answered)

### 📸 Media Sharing
- Photos, videos, contacts, and locations
- Live Photos (iOS) and Motion Photos (Android) support
- Animated and seasonal stickers
- GIFs and custom animated stickers

### 📎 Files & Attachments
- Documents (PDF, DOCX, ZIP, etc.)
- Built-in document scanning (scan/crop PDF/image) on iPhone and Android
- Drag-and-drop file sharing on web and desktop apps

---

## 2. Calling

### 📱 Voice & Video Calls
- One-to-one voice and video calls
- Add participants during a call
- Improved video quality and adaptive bandwidth
- Pinch-to-zoom in video calls
- Scheduled calls and guest chats for users not on the platform

### 👥 Group Calls
- Support for up to ~32 participants (depending on device/version)
- Group voice chats (live audio rooms)
- Speaker spotlight and tappable emoji reactions in calls
- Favorite contacts and call filters

---

## 3. Groups & Communities

### Group Chats
- Large groups with roles (admins, @all mentions)
- Group metadata and member tags
- See who’s online in a group at a glance
- Group polls and decision tools
- Safety overview to prevent unknown group spam

### 📅 Event Tools
- Tools for organising events and RSVPs inside groups

---

## 4. Privacy & Safety

### Presence Controls
- Control visibility of last seen, read receipts, online status, profile photo, about, and status
- Control who can resend your status

### Account Protection
- Block and report
- Two-step verification
- Strict account protection toggle

---

## 5. Chat Management

### 📌 Organisation
- Chat folders (e.g. Work, Friends)
- Favorites tab for chats and calls
- Archived chats

### 🗂️ Search & Sorting
- Smart search across chats, media, and links
- Group chat name-based search

### ⏰ Reminders
- Message reminders for follow-ups

---

## 6. User Experience

- Message reactions (long-press emoji)
- Apple Watch companion support
- Third-party chat interoperability (in testing)
