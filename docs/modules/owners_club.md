# Owners Club & Spare Parts — Feature Description

## Tanzania Context

### Car Owner Communities

Car enthusiast communities in Tanzania are vibrant but entirely informal, operating through WhatsApp groups and occasional physical meetups. These communities serve critical functions beyond socializing — they are the primary way owners share knowledge about maintenance, find trusted mechanics, source parts, and organize group trips.

Active communities in Tanzania:
- **Toyota Club Tanzania** — Largest, given Toyota's market dominance. Sub-groups by model: Land Cruiser, Hilux, Prado, etc.
- **Land Cruiser Club East Africa** — Cross-border community of LC owners, organizes overland expeditions
- **Subaru Club Tanzania** — Active group of Subaru enthusiasts, organizes rallies
- **BMW Club Tanzania** — Premium car enthusiasts
- **Mercedes-Benz Club Tanzania** — Luxury car owners
- **Jeep/4x4 Club Tanzania** — Off-road enthusiasts
- **Boda Boda communities** — Motorcycle owner groups (massive market)
- **Trucks/Commercial vehicle groups** — Transport business owners

These WhatsApp groups typically have 50-256 members and serve as informal marketplaces, advice forums, and social groups simultaneously. Limitations: no searchable history, no organized knowledge base, spam and off-topic content, no verification of advice quality, and membership is invitation-only.

### Spare Parts Market

The spare parts market in Tanzania is enormous and largely informal. Most parts enter the country through:

**Import Channels**
- **Japan** — "Cut" parts (parts from dismantled vehicles), green channel imports, new aftermarket
- **Dubai/UAE** — Both genuine and aftermarket parts, faster shipping
- **China** — Cheapest aftermarket parts, quality concerns
- **UK** — Parts for British-market vehicles
- **Local agents** — Companies like Toyota Tanzania (genuine parts), Mantrac, Auto Parts Centre

**Local Markets**
- **Kariakoo Market (Dar es Salaam)** — Massive spare parts market area with hundreds of shops
- **Tandika/Mbagala** — Used parts from dismantled vehicles
- **Uwanja wa Fisi** — Secondhand parts market
- Each major city has its own spare parts area (Arusha: Central Market area, Mwanza, Dodoma)

**Pain Points**
- Counterfeit parts are rampant — fake Toyota, Honda, etc. parts sold as genuine
- No way to verify part authenticity before purchase
- Compatibility confusion — wrong parts for specific model years
- Price gouging for rare parts
- No returns policy in informal markets
- Finding specific parts requires visiting multiple shops physically
- No delivery infrastructure — you must go to the market
- Salvage/used part quality is unpredictable
- No warranty on parts purchased informally

## International Reference Apps

1. **RockAuto (USA)** — Massive online auto parts store. Search by year/make/model, genuine and aftermarket options, price comparison across brands, detailed fitment information, customer reviews per part, photos, shipping worldwide.

2. **Pelican Parts (USA)** — European car parts specialist. OEM and aftermarket, technical articles, installation guides, community forums, vehicle-specific catalogs, DIY repair guides with photos.

3. **Reddit Car Communities** (r/cars, r/MechanicAdvice, r/Toyota, etc.) — Discussion forums organized by topic and brand, expert and community advice, photo/video sharing, problem diagnosis, modification showcases, parts recommendations.

4. **PartSouq (UAE/Middle East)** — OEM parts catalog for Middle East market. VIN-based parts lookup, exploded diagrams showing part location, genuine parts ordering, delivery across Africa and Middle East.

5. **CarParts.com / AutoZone (USA)** — Consumer auto parts. Search by vehicle, symptom-based part finder, installation videos, store pickup or delivery, part compatibility guarantee, rewards programs.

## TAJIRI Integration Points

- **Wallet (WalletService)** — Parts purchases via `WalletService.deposit()` with M-Pesa/Tigo/Airtel STK push. Community event fees (meetups, car shows, rallies) paid through wallet. Premium community memberships with subscription payments. Escrow for high-value parts transactions via `WalletService.transfer()`. Parts seller payouts processed through wallet. Transaction history via `WalletService.getTransactions()` shows all parts purchases and community fees
- **My Cars Module (my_cars/)** — Auto-join relevant brand/model communities based on registered vehicles. Parts compatibility verification using vehicle specs (make, model, year, engine, VIN) from My Cars. Vehicle showcase profile auto-populated with data from My Cars garage. Mileage milestones tracked and shared to community. Modification log synced with vehicle records. Service history shared in knowledge base discussions
- **Service Garage Module (service_garage/)** — Book professional installation for purchased parts directly from parts listing. Mechanic recommendations from community discussions linked to garage profiles. Community-verified garage ratings. DIY installation guide linked to garage booking as fallback. Torque specifications and technical data from community referenced during service
- **Groups (GroupService)** — Owners clubs built on `GroupService.createGroup()` infrastructure with extended features. Brand-specific groups (Toyota Club, BMW Club, Subaru Club) and model-specific sub-groups (Land Cruiser Owners, Hilux Owners). Community management via `GroupService.getMembers()` with role assignment (admin, expert, founding member). `GroupService.inviteUsers()` for community growth. `GroupService.getGroupPosts()` powering community feed with discussions, knowledge base, and marketplace
- **Messaging (MessageService)** — Member-to-member communication via `MessageService.sendMessage()` for advice, parts inquiries, and trip planning. Buyer-seller parts negotiation chat. `MessageService.createGroup()` for sub-community chats (expedition teams, rally groups). Quick advice requests to expert members
- **Notifications (FCMService + NotificationService)** — Push notifications via `FCMService` for: new community posts, event announcements, parts availability alerts, price drop notifications on watched parts, community event reminders, new member introductions, question answers, vehicle recall alerts relevant to your registered vehicles, mileage milestone celebrations
- **Profile (ProfileService)** — Car enthusiast badges on TAJIRI profile via `ProfileService.getProfile()`. Community roles displayed (founding member, expert, moderator). Seller verification for parts marketplace participants. Vehicle showcase linked from profile. Community participation score and contribution history
- **Events Module (events/)** — Community meetup and car show event management using full events system (committee, budget, tickets, michango). Group drives and off-road expedition planning with RSVP and route sharing. Rally organization with participant management. Car show competitions with community voting. Training workshops at partnered garages
- **Buy Car Module (buy_car/)** — Community advice on vehicles being considered for purchase — ask owners about reliability, common issues, and running costs. Model-specific buying guides in knowledge base. Owner experience posts referenced in vehicle listings. Pre-purchase advice threads
- **Sell Car Module (sell_car/)** — Announce vehicle sale to brand community members first via community post — reaches motivated buyers who understand and want the model. Community members vouch for vehicle condition. Sale listing shared with community marketplace. Model-specific pricing advice from experienced owners
- **Tajirika Module (tajirika/)** — Spare parts dealers and importers registered as TAJIRI partners with verification badges. Parts shop owners in dealer directory linked to partner profiles. Mechanic partners recommended by community. Commission tracking for partner sales
- **Posts & Stories (PostService + StoryService)** — Share vehicle showcase posts to main TAJIRI feed via `PostService.createPost()`. Road trip photo stories via `StoryService.createStory()`. Before/after modification posts. Community event highlights. Parts review posts with photos. `PostService.likePost()`, `PostService.sharePost()`, `PostService.commentOnPost()` for engagement on community content
- **Shop (ShopService)** — Spare parts integrated into TAJIRI marketplace via `ShopService`. Parts listings accessible through shop browse. Cart and checkout via `ShopService` for multi-seller parts ordering. Product reviews via shop review system. Order tracking through shop infrastructure
- **Location (LocationService)** — Community event locations via `LocationService.searchLocations()` using Tanzania hierarchy. Nearby parts shops and dealers on map. Group drive route planning. Regional sub-group assignment based on user location
- **Calendar (CalendarService)** — Community events synced to calendar via `CalendarService.createEvent()`. Group drive dates. Car show schedules. Parts delivery estimates. Vehicle milestone dates
- **Media (PhotoService + VideoUploadService)** — Vehicle showcase galleries uploaded via `PhotoService.uploadPhoto()`. Modification before/after photos. Trip documentation with route photos. Parts listing photos. Video walkarounds via `VideoUploadService`. Community event photo albums via `PhotoService.createAlbum()`
- **People Search (PeopleSearchService)** — Find community members by vehicle model, location, or expertise via `PeopleSearchService.search()`. Discover mutual friends in car communities. Find expert mechanics recommended by community
- **Presence (PresenceService)** — Show community member online status via `PresenceService` for real-time advice requests. Expert member availability for quick questions
- **Content Discovery (ContentEngineService + HashtagService)** — Community content discoverable through personalized recommendations. Vehicle-specific hashtags (#LandCruiserTZ, #SubaruClubTZ) via `HashtagService`. Trending modifications and parts. Cross-community content discovery
- **Real-time Updates (LiveUpdateService)** — Firestore listeners for real-time community feed updates. New post notifications. Parts listing price changes. Event attendance updates
- **Friends (FriendService)** — See friends in same community via `FriendService.getMutualFriends()`. Friend referrals to join communities. Social proof — "5 friends are in Toyota Club Tanzania"
- **Spare Parts Module (spare_parts/)** — Deep integration with standalone spare parts marketplace. Parts search pre-filled with community vehicle specs. Community-recommended sellers highlighted. Counterfeit alert system powered by community reports. Parts availability notifications for community members' vehicles
